About ICIET 2027

Where Intelligence Meets Connectivity

The International Conference on Intelligent Electronics and Telecommunications (ICIET 2027) is a premier international forum for researchers, engineers, and practitioners working at the intersection of intelligent electronics, signal processing, and next-generation telecommunications.

Hosted by the University Mohamed El Bachir El Ibrahimi of Bordj Bou Arreridj in Algeria, ICIET 2027 brings together leading experts from academia and industry to share breakthrough research, exchange ideas, and chart the future of smart electronic systems and communication networks.

The conference will feature peer-reviewed paper presentations, invited keynote lectures, special sessions, and workshops covering the most critical challenges and opportunities in intelligent electronics and telecommunications today. Every submission undergoes rigorous double-blind peer review by at least three independent reviewers with no conflict of interest, in line with international scientific publishing standards.

Accepted papers will be submitted for possible inclusion in IEEE Xplore, subject to meeting IEEE Xplore's scope and quality requirements.

Call for Papers

Topics of Interest

Authors are invited to submit original, unpublished research in — but not limited to — the following areas:

T01

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning in Electronics

Deep learning, neural networks, AI-driven circuit design and optimization.

T02

5G/6G and Next-Generation Wireless Networks

Massive MIMO, mmWave, network slicing, and beyond-5G architectures.

T03

Internet of Things (IoT) and Embedded Systems

Edge computing, sensor networks, energy-efficient IoT protocols.

T04

Signal Processing and Communications

Digital signal processing, OFDM, channel estimation, coding techniques.

T05

Antenna Design and Microwave Engineering

Reconfigurable antennas, phased arrays, metamaterials, RF circuits.

T06

Cybersecurity in Telecommunications

Network security, intrusion detection, cryptographic protocols for communications.

T07

Power Electronics and Energy Harvesting

Smart grids, renewable energy systems, wireless power transfer.

T08

Computer Vision and Image Processing

Object detection, medical imaging, intelligent surveillance systems.

T09

Optical Communications and Photonics

Fiber optic systems, visible light communication, integrated photonics.

T10

VLSI Design and Nanoelectronics

Chip design, low-power architectures, nanoscale fabrication technologies.

T11

Robotics and Autonomous Systems

Control systems, autonomous vehicles, human-robot interaction.

T12

Smart Cities and Intelligent Transportation

V2X communications, smart infrastructure, intelligent mobility systems.

Paper Submission

01

Prepare Your Manuscript

Format your paper using the IEEE conference template (A4 or US Letter). Papers must be written in English and should represent original, unpublished work not currently under review elsewhere.

02

Check Paper Length

Regular papers should be 5–6 pages. Extended papers up to 8 pages are accepted with an additional fee. Short papers (2–4 pages) are welcome for work-in-progress contributions.

03

Blind Review Compliance

Submissions undergo double-blind peer review by at least three independent reviewers with no conflict of interest. Remove all author information and acknowledgments from your initial submission. Author details are included only in the final camera-ready version.

04

Submit via the Portal

Upload your PDF through the official submission system (EasyChair / EDAS — link to be announced). You will receive a confirmation email upon successful submission.

05

Registration & Presentation

At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present the paper at the conference. Papers without a registered author will be removed from the proceedings.

Conference Venue

University of BBA

ICIET 2027 will be held at the University Mohamed El Bachir El Ibrahimi of Bordj Bou Arreridj, located in Bordj Bou Arreridj, Algeria. The university is one of the leading higher education institutions in northeastern Algeria, offering modern conference facilities and a vibrant academic environment.

Bordj Bou Arreridj (BBA) is a city in northeastern Algeria, accessible by road and rail, situated in a fertile highland region. It is approximately 230 km from Algiers.
